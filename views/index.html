ATAA' SYSTEM

  • Donate

    Ataa' system will allow user to donate their food, clothes and furniture to the one who need it easily.

  • Collect

    Ataa' system will link the donor to the charity to collect the item.   

  • Receive

    Ataa' system will link the charity to receiver to give him the item.   

  • Notify

    Ataa' system will notify the donor that his donation is done.
